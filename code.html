Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking in the Himalayas

What is the best time to visit Uttarakhand? expand_more
Uttarakhand is a year-round destination. For snow lovers, December to February is ideal. For pleasant weather and trekking, March to June and September to November are perfect.
Are the homestays pet-friendly? expand_more
Many of our listed properties are pet-friendly! Look for the "Pet Friendly" tag on the property card or use our filters to find suitable accommodations for your furry friends.
Is internet connectivity good in remote areas? expand_more
Most of our properties offer high-speed WiFi, making them great for workcations. However, some remote off-grid cabins might have limited connectivity. Please check the specific amenities listed for each property.
What is the cancellation policy? expand_more
Cancellation policies vary by host. You can find flexible options that allow free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in. Review the booking details carefully before confirming.
